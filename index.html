Game Concept

Pollution Solution is a fun serious game about cleaning up air and water pollution. It is a 2D side-scrolling platform game where the player avoids pollution, collects filter parts and saves the world.

Story

The FACTORY is polluting the environment with air and water pollution. As an environmental hero, the player's task is to avoid being poisoned by the pollution, collect the items required to craft a filter for the FACTORY, and install the filter in the FACTORY to complete the level.

Along the way, the main character cleans up the environment by removing the pollutants (enemies). This is achieved by the use of an 'air gun'. The gun can be fired at 'enemies' (smog and oil), to clean the environment.

The level slowly fills with smog and water pollution. If the player cannot remove the pollutants and install the factory filter in time, the screen goes black, and the player dies.

Environment and Level Design

Terrain types - platforms on land and underwater; Some platforms may move; There will be pressure pads to open doors/trapdoors in the level.

Player

An environmental warrior that wants to clean up the environment, and educate the player about the dangers of water and air pollution.

Movement - Left/right, Swim, (Special ability: double-jump or dash)

Player variables - Health Bar: affected by lack of air, poison, and touching enemies.

Enemies

Main enemy (Boss) - the FACTORY (A factory spewing out air and water pollution), Water enemies - Oil (Blobs of oil/slick in the water), Land/air enemies - Air pollution (smog clouds)

Enemy movement - Left/right/up/down, simple AI (patrolling system/random movement)

Enemy properties - Toxic to main player (player touches enemy, reduces health)

Collectibles/Items

Air (from canisters), Pieces of filter (to be collected and combined into LARGE FILTER to complete the level), Health pickups

UI elements

Splash Screen: Start Game, Resume (Pause Screen), Quit

HUD elements (visual)

Health Bar Air Supply (when air ==0, reduces health), Poison (slowly drains the player of health) - screen brightness and amount of pollution in the air/water.

Scope

As a small team on an ambitious deadline, we are limiting the scope to delivering one complete level of the game.

Platform

The platform will initially be the PC desktop, with possible expansion to mobile platforms later.
